Understanding the Classes

CN - Cognitively Normal Individuals with normal cognitive function and no signs of dementia visible in the scan structure.
MCI - Mild Cognitive Impairment An intermediate stage between expected cognitive decline of normal aging and the more serious decline of dementia.
AD - Alzheimer's Disease Pathological changes indicative of Alzheimer's dementia, including significant atrophy in the hippocampus and other regions.

About NeuroScan AI

NeuroScan AI is a cutting-edge demonstration of using 3D Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) for the early detection of Alzheimer's Disease.

How it Works

The system analyzes structural MRI waveforms to identify biomarkers associated with three key stages:

  • CN (Cognitively Normal): Healthy brain structure.
  • MCI (Mild Cognitive Impairment): Intermediate stage, often a precursor to dementia.
  • AD (Alzheimer's Disease): Advanced neurodegeneration.
Disclaimer: This tool is for research and educational purposes only. The current version runs on a demonstration model trained with mock data and should not be used for actual medical diagnosis.
